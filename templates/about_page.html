Singapore’s education system puts a large emphasis that subject content is not the only thing students would learn in Singapore. One crucial part of the education system wokd be how all schools have Education and Career Guidance (ECG) session, to guide the students in their possible career options and their ambitions. Our careers do have a reliance on the path e take in our education, thus ECG is important. MOE has implemented ECG in all schools with the objectives of:

  1. Nurture students' self-awareness, self-directedness and life skills for continuous learning.
  2. Provide students with accurate and comprehensive information and enable them to explore well-thought through education and career options.
  3. Instil in students an appreciation for the value of all occupations and how they contribute to the society.
  4. Equip students with skills to positively engage their parents and other career influencers such as teachers, trusted adults and industry experts.

With such a huge emphasis on ECG in schools, we decided to do a survey to find out how students in their teenage years feel about their career path, their thoughts about the help that they have on ECG, as well as what they think. Would help them in better understanding their career path through ECG.
